# Exposed RDP defensive guidance

Exposed RDP should be reviewed as a high-priority access-control and secure
configuration concern. Confirm whether remote access is approved, restrict
access through controlled pathways, require strong authentication, and monitor
successful and failed logon events.

# Remediation tracking

Record asset owner, exposure justification, compensating controls, and the date
the exposure was reduced or accepted.
