# Response guidance

When a signal is suspicious, preserve the source evidence, identify affected
assets, validate business impact, and coordinate response through approved
incident handling procedures.

# Recovery guidance

Track containment and remediation actions, then confirm that monitoring remains
in place after the change. Avoid drawing conclusions that are not supported by
stored evidence.
