# Defensive exposure triage

When a finding indicates exposed infrastructure, validate ownership, business
purpose, exposure history, and whether the service should be internet reachable.
Use passive evidence, asset inventory, and approved configuration records before
assigning severity.

# Defensive context

MITRE ATT&CK context can help describe how exposed assets may be observed during
reconnaissance, but the response should stay defensive: confirm scope, reduce
unnecessary exposure, improve logging, and document remediation status.
